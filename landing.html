Welcome to our Campus Community Course!

 

LBJ ECHS Jaguar Community:
As the coronavirus (COVID-19) continues to spread; we are continuing to take significant steps to prevent the spread on our campus and protect the health & well-being of our students, staff, and families.
The school will remain closed through April 13th, and we will discontinue on-campus instruction and transition to a remote learning environment for all students. This could extend further depending on information provided by our district headquarters, and additional guidance will be provided accordingly.
 
The transition to remote learning will assure that there is limited interruption to students’ academic progress, and will remain in guidelines with the District & TEA.
 
***We will provide ongoing support and guidance on this huge transition, and remember we are all in this together as we support one another during this challenging time! #JaguarFamily
 
Connecting with Families in Need:
Campus Administration will work with C.I.S., Family Resource Center, VIDA, and other campus Service Providers to identify our families in high need to support accordingly. We will make a diligent effort to engage all of our families to ensure they are fully acclimated with available resources, informed, and remain connected during these times. Please complete this survey so we can gather data about how we can support you.
