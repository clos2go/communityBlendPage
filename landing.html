Welcome back to our LBJ Jaguar ECHS Campus Community Course!

The purpose of this course is to provide a place to share information with students, parents, and staff in BLEND.

BLEND is a Learning Management System (LMS) designed to support and amplify teaching, learning, and communication in Austin ISD. BLEND is our district's version of Canvas, a digital tool that is being used by schools and universities across the country to provide students with a 21st-century educational experience.
